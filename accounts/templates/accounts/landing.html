What we expect from our Online Essay Writers

  • Academic writing skills

    • All our writers are required to have the necessary academic writing skills. The writers are expected to know the variety of writing formats. The standard writing styles are MLA, APA, Harvard, and Chicago/Turbian. You are required to keep up with any modifications on these writing styles.

  • Ability to meet deadlines

    • Our writers are required to be dedicated to their work; they should be able to be quick and accurate typist. As our writer, you should be able to meet short deadlines and still maintain high quality in the paper you have done. You should always be ready to work under strict deadlines.

  • Research skills

    • Our writers are required to have exceptional research skills, and you should have the ability to read and understand the content quickly. They should be able to compile a variety of data and quickly translate it into their words. Having access to an academic library is an added advantage as a freelance writer.

  • Honesty & Commitment

    • We require complete honesty from our writers; our writers should be able to deliver plagiarism free papers that they wrote from scratch. They should also be honest with the clients on their ability to complete the academic paper. They should also be honest on when they will be able to deliver it.

  • At least Bachelor's degree

    • We only recruited individuals who have achieved top academic levels. This is to ensure that we get the best scholars to work for us. Copies of your academic credentials are required during application to be part of our writing team. We require complete honesty during submission of your request.

  • Responsibility

    • We would like our writers to be responsible in the way they handle our clients and carry themselves. We only employ the most professional writers. You can be an excellent writer, but your character may be wanting. We only hire writers who understand the value of being responsible for their action, courteous and professional.

  • Effective Communication Skills

    • We never expect our writers to be online at all times; we are all-human and cannot work throughout the day. However, we do expect that you will quickly respond to the client and attend to what is required of you. It can be very disturbing to the customer when the writer does not respond to their email.
